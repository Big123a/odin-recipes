Banh Mi Sliders

Description

Banh Mi Sliders Vietnamese-inspired pork and beef banh mi sliders. Spread with Sriracha-cilantro aioli and top with daikon cucumber relish and pickled carrots.


Ingredients

  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • ½ pound ground pork
  • ½ cup rolled oats
  • 1 slice bread, shredded
  • 1 large egg
  • ¼ small onion, diced
  • 1 stalk green onion, sliced
  • 3 tablespoons dry onion soup mix
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh ginger root
  • ½ tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 (15 ounce) package slider-size burger buns
